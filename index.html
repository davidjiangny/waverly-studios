  • Project Managers

    The Project Manager is the primary Creative Group contact for all digital projects. All digital requests that come in to the Creative Group will be filtered through the Project Manager. This person will be responsible for managing these requests, and working collaboratively with label creative managers, design directors, art directors, video producers and media to ensure aligned communication within the group and to meet client deadlines.

  • Social Media Managers

    We are seeking Social Media Managers who will help devise Social Media marketing plans for the company. Administration includes developing content strategy; brand awareness and generating inbound traffic. The Social Media Account Manager should be a highly motivated individual with experience and a passion for designing and implementing content strategy, creating relevant content, monitoring and analyzing trends and devising social campaigns based on client's trends.

  • Creative Directors

    This dynamic position plays an essential role in the marketing, creative, and communications. This person is responsible for a wide range of designing tasks from concept to development including; print collateral, websites, video, events, and related items. This person reports directly to the Founders and works in harmony with other departments within the organization to meet time frames.

    Find out more

  • Influencer Managers

    This role is a key part of our User Acquisition strategy - building a network of influencers across social platforms to build awareness amongst our core audience. It will be your job to cultivate relationships with influencers. Especially important in this role will be an inherent affinity with this audience, being able to communicate and build relationships effectively. An additional but equally important aspect to this role will be to build and manage our own community of users and ambassadors.
